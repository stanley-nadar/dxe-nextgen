# Footer

The standard footer component. There is an option to render it in black for a reserved page.

## Fields

#### Contact Number

Takes any valid international number and renders it in the contact bar next to the Contact Email.

#### Contact Email

Takes any email address and renders it in the contact bar next to the Contact Number.

#### Menu Items

Takes references to the Menu Items content model. Renders out the Menu Items as navigable lists.

#### Announcement Card

An optional Menu Item reference that if present renders a card with an Image and its own CTA.

#### Google Play URL & Apple Store URL

Optional URL's that if present render a button to each of the URL's destinations. (Google url a Google Play button and
Apple Store is an Apple Store button)

#### Privacy & Terms Menu

Takes a list of up to 7 menu items that are rendered at the bottom. These are typically your privacy information and
legal text links.

#### Company

This is Freshworks. Since important company info can be referenced in multiple places, it is set into a content item of
its own and referenced throughout the site. This is where the footer gets the information for the social navigation.

#### Copyright

Legal text at the bottom of the footer.
